About Us

About Us – Pakistan Film Society of Islamabad 

We’re just two filmmakers who got tired of waiting for the perfect cinema culture—so we built it ourselves.

Hi, we’re Nargis and Moneeb. Back in 2020, over one too many chai sessions complaining about how Pakistani films deserved better audiences (and audiences deserved better films), we dragged some cushions into the I-8 Community Library, borrowed a projector, and screened our first movie for 12 friends. That night—with the hum of the generator, the smell of fresh popcorn, and the way complete strangers started debating the ending like old friends—we knew this had to grow.

Today, what started as a reckless passion project has become a home for Islamabad’s film lovers. Our screenings aren’t about red carpets or VIP seats—they’re about that magic moment when the lights dim and 300 strangers suddenly feel like family. We champion the films that make you cry in public, the young filmmakers who dare to experiment, and the radical idea that great cinema belongs to everyone—not just those who can afford multiplex tickets.

Bring Everyone Together 

Come as you are. Bring your grandma who misses the Lahore cinemas of her youth, your friend who “doesn’t read subtitles,” and that cousin who still argues about Maula Jatt. We promise: by the time the credits roll, you’ll remember why falling in love with movies feels like coming home.

Social media outreach 100,000+ monthly views.

It developed from genuine discussions on the movies that touch us, not by following trends, to reach over 100,000+ views every month. We don't just share schedules; we share stories. A young filmmaker finding their first audience or a student finding comfort in a scene that resonates to their own unsaid feelings are just two examples of the people who stopped to connect behind every view.
Numbers aren't the point here. It's about the quiet moments: the messages sent late at night after a screening, the random people who end up working together, and how a single frame can give someone a sense of visibility. We are listening when we post, not blasting.
Come along. For the kind of movie that lingers in your mind long after the screen goes dark, not for the content.
Keep up with instagram @isbfilmsociety.

More than 2,000 members by encouraging inclusivity, accessibility, and a love of movies.

By providing reasonably priced, inclusive movie screenings, workshops, and networking opportunities, the Pakistan Film Society of Islamabad attracted more than 2000+ registered members. By offering inexpensive tickets (Rs. 250), stimulating conversations, and social media promotion, we created a vibrant community of filmmakers, students, and moviegoers—reviving the passion for cinema in Pakistan.

Text: Nargis & Moneeb
