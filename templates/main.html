Pakistan Film Society

Pakistan Film Society of Islamabad Reviving Pakistani Cinema, One Screening At a Time.

Pakistan Film Society of Islamabad is a grassroots cinematic movement dedicated to reviving Pakistan's film culture through accessible, community-driven experiences. Founded by filmmakers Nargis and Moneeb we organize affordable film screenings.
